Welcome To Icon's Bistro | Coffee & Collaboration

Little Story About Us

Icon's Bistro | Coffee & Collaboration was founded in 2019. Our place has the concept of a combination of a cafe and an automotive garage. Our place has 2 floors where the 1st floor is for the automotive garage and the 2nd floor is for the cafe.

Since its inception until now, Icon's Bistro | Coffee & Collaboration has 3 branches located in Jakarta, Surabaya and Jember.
